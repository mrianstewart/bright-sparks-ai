This privacy policy explains how Bright Sparks AI ("I", "me") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you visit brightsparks.ai or sign up for the newsletter. I take your privacy seriously and will only use your data as described here.

1. What data I collect

The only personal data I collect is your email address, and only when you voluntarily submit it via the newsletter signup form on this site.

I do not collect names, payment details, or any other personal data through this site.

2. Why I collect it

Your email address is collected solely to:

  • Send you the weekly Bright Sparks AI newsletter (new builds, breakdowns, and updates)
  • Occasionally notify you about new products or free tools

I will not use your email for any other purpose, and I will never sell or share it with third parties for marketing.

3. How it's stored

Email addresses are stored and processed by Kit (formerly ConvertKit), a US-based email marketing platform. Kit acts as a data processor on my behalf. You can read Kit's privacy policy at kit.com/privacy.

Kit participates in the EU–US Data Privacy Framework, which provides an adequate level of protection for personal data transferred from the UK/EEA to the US.

4. Your rights under UK GDPR

Under UK data protection law, you have the following rights:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the personal data I hold about you.
  • Deletion: You can ask me to delete your email address at any time. You can also unsubscribe via the link in any newsletter.
  • Correction: If your email address changes, you can ask me to update it.
  • Portability: You can request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: You can object to processing of your data for direct marketing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email me at hello@brightsparks.ai. I will respond within 30 days.

If you are unhappy with how I handle your data, you have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

5. Legal basis for processing

I process your email address on the basis of consent — you freely gave it by submitting the signup form. You can withdraw that consent at any time by unsubscribing.

6. Cookies and analytics

This site uses Plausible Analytics, a privacy-first analytics tool. Plausible does not use cookies and does not track individual users. It collects only aggregated, anonymous statistics (e.g. page view counts, referral sources). No personal data is processed by the analytics script, and no consent banner is required.

No other cookies are set by this site.

7. Data retention

I will hold your email address for as long as you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe or request deletion, your email will be removed from the mailing list promptly (within 30 days at most).
